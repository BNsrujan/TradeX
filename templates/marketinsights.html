The Power of Our Charting Platform – Advanced Patterns

To streamline your analysis, our charting platform offers one-click identification of various technical patterns. Below is an in-depth look at these patterns:

1. SR Zones (Support & Resistance Zones)
Identify key levels where the price historically reverses or consolidates to help determine entry and exit points.
2. Trendlines
Draw straight lines connecting significant highs or lows to highlight the market's directional bias and spot potential reversals.
3. Parallel Channels
Detect channels that contain price action, allowing you to gauge trend strength and predict breakouts or reversals when prices reach channel boundaries.
4. Triangles
Recognize triangular formations where price ranges narrow over time, often signaling trend continuation or an impending breakout.
5. Double Tops and Bottoms
Spot reversal patterns where the price tests a high or low level twice before changing direction—an indicator that a trend reversal might be underway.
6. V-Shape
Detect rapid reversals marked by a steep decline followed by an equally steep recovery, often presenting a sharp trading opportunity.
7. Inside Bars
Recognize consolidation patterns where the current bar’s range is completely within the previous bar’s range, often preceding significant moves.
8. Bollinger Bands
Visualize volatility with bands that expand and contract based on market fluctuations, helping you identify overbought or oversold conditions.
9. Morning and Evening Stars
Detect candlestick reversal patterns where the Morning Star signals a bullish reversal and the Evening Star indicates a bearish reversal.
